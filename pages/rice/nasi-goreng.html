NASI GORENG

Ingredients

For the Rice:

  • 3 cups cooked and cooled jasmine rice
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 red chili (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp shrimp paste (optional)

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp fish sauce (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper

For the Toppings:

  • 2 eggs (fried sunny side up)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken or shrimp
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup sliced cucumber and tomato (for garnish)
  • Prawn crackers (optional)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Ingredients:

  1. Cook the jasmine rice and let it cool completely before frying.
  2. Chop the onion, garlic, and chili.
  3. Mix the sauce ingredients in a small bowl.

2. Cook the Aromatics:

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic, onion, and chili, and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp paste (if using) and stir-fry for another minute.

3. Fry the Rice:

  1. Add the cooked chicken or shrimp and stir well.
  2. Add the cooled rice and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Pour in the prepared sauce and mix thoroughly to coat the rice evenly.
  4. Cook for another 2 minutes while stirring continuously.

4. Serve the Nasi Goreng:

  1. Divide the fried rice into serving plates.
  2. Top each serving with a fried egg.
  3. Garnish with sliced cucumber, tomato, and green onions.
  4. Serve with prawn crackers if desired.

5. Enjoy!

  • Serve hot and enjoy this authentic Indonesian fried rice!
