CHICKEN RICE

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 lbs)
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 4 slices ginger
  • 3 stalks green onions
  • Water (enough to cover the chicken)

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 2 tbsp chicken fat or vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 slices ginger
  • 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 tsp salt

For the Sauces:

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger
  • 1 tbsp garlic (minced)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Chicken:

  1. Rub the chicken with salt to clean and exfoliate the skin.
  2. Rinse and place in a large pot with water, ginger, and green onions.
  3. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 45 minutes until the chicken is cooked.
  4. Remove the chicken and place it in an ice bath to stop cooking and keep the skin firm.

2. Cook the Rice:

  1. In a pan, heat chicken fat or oil over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add jasmine rice and stir well to coat with the flavors.
  4. Transfer the rice to a rice cooker, add chicken broth, and cook as usual.

3. Prepare the Sauces:

  1. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce and sesame oil for a simple dressing.
  2. In another bowl, combine chili sauce, minced ginger, and garlic.

4. Serve the Chicken Rice:

  1. Slice the poached chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Serve with the fragrant rice, dipping sauces, and sliced cucumbers.
  3. Enjoy with a bowl of the remaining chicken broth.
