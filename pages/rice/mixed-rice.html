MIX FRIED RICE

Ingredients

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups cooked and cooled jasmine rice
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions

For the Protein:

  • 1/2 cup shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 1/2 cup squid rings
  • 1/2 cup fish fillet (cut into small pieces)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken (shredded)
  • 1/2 cup beef (thinly sliced)
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

1. Prepare the Ingredients:

  1. Cook the jasmine rice and let it cool completely before frying.
  2. Chop the onion, garlic, and green onions.
  3. Season the shrimp, squid, fish, chicken, and beef with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.

2. Cook the Proteins:

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok or large pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add shrimp, squid, fish, chicken, and beef, and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until cooked.
  3. Push everything to one side of the pan and pour in the beaten eggs.
  4. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked, then mix with the proteins.
  5. Remove everything from the pan and set aside.

3. Stir-Fry the Rice:

  1. Heat the remaining oil in the same pan.
  2. Add garlic and onion, sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the cooled rice and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce, white pepper, and salt.
  6. Mix well to ensure even coating of the sauces.

4. Combine Everything:

  1. Add the cooked proteins and eggs back into the pan and mix well.
  2. Stir in the chopped green onions.

5. Serve and Enjoy!

  • Serve hot and enjoy your delicious mixed fried rice!
