KIMCHI FRIED RICE

Ingredients

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups cooked and cooled jasmine rice
  • 1 cup kimchi (chopped, with juice)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (optional: carrots, peas, corn)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds

For the Protein (Optional):

  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken (shredded) or beef (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 cup tofu (cubed and pan-fried)
  • 1 egg (fried, for topping)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

1. Prepare the Ingredients:

  1. Cook the jasmine rice and let it cool completely before frying.
  2. Chop the onion, garlic, green onions, and kimchi.

2. Stir-Fry the Rice:

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and onion, sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped kimchi and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in mixed vegetables (if using) and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add the cooled rice and mix well.
  6. Pour in soy sauce, gochujang, white pepper, and salt.
  7. Mix everything thoroughly until evenly coated.

3. Add Protein (Optional):

  1. If using chicken, beef, or tofu, add them to the pan and stir well.
  2. Cook for 2-3 minutes until heated through.

4. Finish and Serve:

  1. Drizzle sesame oil over the rice and mix well.
  2. Top with a fried egg, chopped green onions, and toasted sesame seeds.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious kimchi fried rice!
