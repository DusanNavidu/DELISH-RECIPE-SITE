BIRIYANI

Ingredients

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice (soaked for 30 mins)
  • 4 cups water
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 500g chicken (bone-in preferred)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste

For the Biryani Masala:

  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 2 onions (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander & mint leaves

For Layering & Garnishing:

  • Saffron strands soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk (optional)
  • 2 tbsp fried onions
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Chopped coriander & mint

Instructions

1. Cook the Rice:

  1. In a pot, boil water with cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, bay leaf, and salt.
  2. Add the soaked rice and cook until it's 70-80% cooked (grains should be firm).
  3. Drain and set aside.

2. Marinate the Chicken:

  1. Mix all marinade ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Coat the chicken well and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for best flavor).

3. Prepare the Biryani Masala:

  1. Heat oil/ghee in a pan, add sliced onions, and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add cumin seeds, ginger-garlic paste, and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and cook until tomatoes soften.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook for 10-15 minutes until it's partially cooked.

4. Layer the Biryani:

  1. In a heavy-bottomed pot, spread a layer of cooked chicken.
  2. Add half of the rice on top.
  3. Sprinkle saffron milk, fried onions, coriander, mint, and a spoonful of ghee.
  4. Repeat another layer of rice and top with garnishing.

5. Dum Cooking (Slow Cooking):

  1. Cover the pot with a tight lid.
  2. Cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes (or place a heavy pan under the pot to prevent burning).

6. Serve & Enjoy!

  • Gently mix before serving.
  • Serve hot with raita or salad.
