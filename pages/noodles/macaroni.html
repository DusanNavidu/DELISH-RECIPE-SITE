MACARONI

Ingredients

For the Pasta:

  • 200g macaroni pasta
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1/2 tsp oregano or basil

For the Protein (Optional):

  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken (shredded) or ground beef
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers (chopped)

Instructions

1. Cook the Macaroni:

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Add salt and macaroni.
  3. Cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  4. Drain and set aside.

2. Prepare the Sauce:

  1. Heat butter or olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and garlic, sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, black pepper, and chili flakes.
  4. Stir in heavy cream or milk and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add shredded cheese and mix until melted.

3. Add Protein and Vegetables (Optional):

  1. If using, add cooked chicken, ground beef, mushrooms, or bell peppers.
  2. Stir well and cook for 3-5 minutes.

4. Combine and Serve:

  1. Add cooked macaroni to the sauce and mix well.
  2. Sprinkle oregano or basil on top.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy!
