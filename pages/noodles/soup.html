SOUP

Ingredients

For the Base:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 medium carrot (chopped)
  • 1 celery stalk (chopped)
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme or oregano

For Additional Flavor:

  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken (shredded) or tofu (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup pasta, rice, or potatoes (optional)
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced) or bell peppers (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup spinach or kale (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream or coconut milk (optional for creamy soup)

Instructions

1. Sauté the Vegetables:

  1. Heat olive oil or butter in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onions, garlic, carrots, and celery.
  3. Sauté for 5-7 minutes until soft and fragrant.

2. Add Broth and Seasoning:

  1. Pour in vegetable or chicken broth.
  2. Add diced tomatoes, salt, pepper, and thyme or oregano.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.

3. Add Proteins and Grains:

  1. Stir in cooked chicken, tofu, pasta, rice, or potatoes (if using).
  2. Let cook for another 10 minutes until everything is tender.

4. Final Touches:

  1. Add mushrooms, bell peppers, spinach, or kale.
  2. For a creamy soup, stir in heavy cream or coconut milk.
  3. Simmer for 5 more minutes.

5. Serve and Enjoy:

  1. Ladle the soup into bowls.
  2. Garnish with fresh herbs or grated cheese (optional).
  3. Serve hot with bread or crackers!
