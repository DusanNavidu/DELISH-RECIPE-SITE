PASTA

Ingredients

For the Pasta:

  • 200g pasta (penne, spaghetti, or fusilli)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup tomato sauce or diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or milk (optional for creamy sauce)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan or mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano or basil

For the Protein (Optional):

  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken (shredded) or ground beef
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers (chopped)

Instructions

1. Cook the Pasta:

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Add salt and pasta.
  3. Cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  4. Drain and set aside.

2. Prepare the Sauce:

  1. Heat olive oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and garlic, sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, black pepper, and chili flakes.
  4. Stir in heavy cream or milk for a creamy sauce (optional) and let simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add grated cheese and mix until melted.

3. Add Protein and Vegetables (Optional):

  1. If using, add cooked chicken, ground beef, mushrooms, or bell peppers.
  2. Stir well and cook for 3-5 minutes.

4. Combine and Serve:

  1. Add cooked pasta to the sauce and mix well.
  2. Sprinkle oregano or basil on top.
  3. Serve hot with extra grated cheese on top!
