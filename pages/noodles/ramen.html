RAMEN

Ingredients

For the Broth:

  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1-inch piece ginger (grated)
  • 1 tsp chili paste (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

For the Noodles:

  • 200g ramen noodles
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

For the Toppings:

  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken (shredded) or tofu (cubed and pan-fried)
  • 1 boiled egg (halved)
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup corn kernels
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 sheet nori (seaweed), cut into strips

Instructions

1. Prepare the Broth:

  1. In a pot, heat sesame oil over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, sauté until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the broth and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add soy sauce, miso paste, chili paste (if using), salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer for 10-15 minutes to develop flavors.

2. Cook the Noodles:

  1. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Drain and set aside.

3. Assemble the Ramen:

  1. Divide the cooked noodles into serving bowls.
  2. Pour the hot broth over the noodles.
  3. Arrange the toppings: chicken or tofu, boiled egg, mushrooms, corn, and green onions.
  4. Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds and add nori strips.

4. Serve and Enjoy:

  1. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade ramen!
