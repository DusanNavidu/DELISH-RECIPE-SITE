PIZZA

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 1/4 tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar

For the Sauce:

  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the Toppings:

  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni slices (optional)
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup black olives (sliced)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Dough:

  1. In a bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add flour, olive oil, and salt. Mix until dough forms.
  3. Knead for about 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise for 1 hour until doubled in size.

2. Prepare the Sauce:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, black pepper, and oregano.
  4. Simmer for 5-7 minutes, then set aside.

3. Assemble the Pizza:

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Punch down the dough and roll it out into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Place on a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread prepared sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  6. Add your favorite toppings.

4. Bake and Serve:

  1. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, until the crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly.
  2. Remove from oven and garnish with fresh basil (optional).
  3. Slice and serve hot!
