NOODLES

Ingredients

For the Noodles:

  • 200g noodles (egg noodles or rice noodles)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cabbage, peas)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp chili sauce (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds

For the Protein (Optional):

  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken (shredded) or beef (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 cup shrimp or tofu (cubed and pan-fried)
  • 1 egg (scrambled or fried, for topping)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions

1. Prepare the Ingredients:

  1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  2. Chop the onion, garlic, green onions, and vegetables.

2. Stir-Fry the Noodles:

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and onion, sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked noodles and mix well.
  5. Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce, white pepper, salt, and chili sauce (if using).
  6. Mix everything thoroughly until evenly coated.

3. Add Protein (Optional):

  1. If using chicken, beef, shrimp, or tofu, add them to the pan and stir well.
  2. Cook for 2-3 minutes until heated through.

4. Finish and Serve:

  1. Drizzle sesame oil over the noodles and mix well.
  2. Top with a fried egg, chopped green onions, and toasted sesame seeds.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious noodles!
