BURGER

Ingredients

For the Burger Patties:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the Burger Assembly:

  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup and mustard (optional)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Burger Patties:

  1. In a bowl, combine ground beef, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and Worcestershire sauce.
  2. Mix everything together until well combined. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet or grill over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the patties for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 160°F (71°C).
  5. If using cheese, place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.

2. Assemble the Burger:

  1. Toast the burger buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster.
  2. Place a burger patty on the bottom half of each bun.
  3. Add lettuce, tomato, pickles, and your favorite condiments like ketchup and mustard.
  4. Top with the other half of the bun.

3. Serve and Enjoy:

  1. Serve your delicious homemade burger with fries or a side salad.
