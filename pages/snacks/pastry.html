PASTRY

Ingredients

For the Pastry Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (cold and cubed)
  • 1/4 cup ice water

For the Filling (optional):

  • 1/2 cup fruit jam (like raspberry, apricot, or strawberry)
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Pastry Dough:

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt.
  2. Cut cold butter into small cubes and add to the flour mixture.
  3. Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, work the butter into the flour until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  4. Slowly add ice water, a tablespoon at a time, mixing until the dough just begins to come together.
  5. Form the dough into a disk, wrap in plastic, and chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.

2. Prepare the Filling (Optional):

  1. If using jam, choose your favorite flavor and set it aside. You can also use fresh fruits if preferred.
  2. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

3. Roll and Shape the Pastry:

  1. Once the dough has chilled, roll it out on a floured surface until it is about 1/8 inch thick.
  2. Cut the dough into squares or circles, depending on your preference.
  3. If you are using jam, spoon a small amount of jam in the center of each pastry.
  4. Fold the pastry over the filling to form a triangle or half-moon shape. Seal the edges by pressing them together with a fork.

4. Bake the Pastry:

  1. Brush the top of each pastry with a beaten egg to give it a golden finish.
  2. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the pastries are golden brown and puffed up.

5. Serve and Enjoy:

  1. Let the pastries cool slightly before serving. You can dust them with powdered sugar if desired.
