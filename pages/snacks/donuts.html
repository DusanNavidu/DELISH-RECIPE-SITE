DONUT

Ingredients

For the Donuts:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter (softened)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

For the Glaze (Optional):

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Sprinkles (optional)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Donut Dough:

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, yeast, salt, and nutmeg.
  2. In a separate small bowl, heat milk until warm (about 110°F / 43°C), and add it to the dry ingredients.
  3. Mix in butter, eggs, and warm water. Stir until a dough forms.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-1.5 hours, or until it has doubled in size.

2. Shape the Donuts:

  1. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and turn it out onto a floured surface.
  2. Roll out the dough to about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Use a donut cutter or two round cutters (one large, one small) to cut out donut shapes.
  4. Place the donuts on a floured tray, cover them, and let them rise for an additional 30-45 minutes.

3. Fry the Donuts:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Carefully place the donuts into the hot oil, frying 2-3 donuts at a time to avoid overcrowding.
  3. Fry for 1-2 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  4. Remove the donuts from the oil and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.

4. Prepare the Glaze (Optional):

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract to make a smooth glaze.
  2. Dunk the warm donuts into the glaze, covering the tops completely.
  3. Sprinkle with optional toppings like sprinkles or chocolate chips, if desired.

5. Serve and Enjoy:

  1. Allow the donuts to cool slightly before serving. Enjoy your homemade donuts!
