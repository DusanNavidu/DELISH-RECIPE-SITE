MANGO JUICE

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe mangoes (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 cup cold water or coconut water
  • 1-2 tbsp sugar or honey (optional, depending on sweetness)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Mint leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Mangoes:

  1. Peel and chop the ripe mangoes, removing the pit.
  2. Place the mango pieces into a blender or food processor.

2. Make the Mango Juice:

  1. Add the cold water or coconut water to the blender along with the mango pieces.
  2. Blend until smooth. If the juice is too thick, add a bit more water to adjust the consistency.
  3. Optional: Add sugar or honey to sweeten the juice if needed.

3. Serve and Garnish:

  1. Pour the mango juice into glasses.
  2. Optional: Add ice cubes to chill the juice and garnish with mint leaves for a fresh touch.

4. Enjoy:

  1. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing homemade mango juice!
