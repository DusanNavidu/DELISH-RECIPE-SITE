AVOCADO JUICE

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1-2 cups cold milk (or almond milk for dairy-free option)
  • 2 tbsp honey or sugar (optional, depending on sweetness)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional, for chilling)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Avocado:

  1. Cut the avocado in half and remove the pit.
  2. Scoop out the flesh into a blender or food processor.

2. Make the Avocado Juice:

  1. Add the cold milk, honey (or sugar), and vanilla extract (if using) to the blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. You can adjust the consistency by adding more milk if necessary.
  3. Optional: Add ice cubes for a chilled, refreshing drink.

3. Serve and Enjoy:

  1. Pour the avocado juice into glasses and serve immediately.
  2. Optional: Garnish with mint leaves or a slice of avocado for decoration.
