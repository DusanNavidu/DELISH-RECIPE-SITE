STRAWBERRY MILKSHAKE

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh or frozen strawberries
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Fresh strawberries (for garnish, optional)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Milkshake:

  1. In a blender, add the strawberries, vanilla ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract (if using).
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. If the milkshake is too thick, add a little more milk and blend again.

2. Serve the Milkshake:

  1. Pour the milkshake into a tall glass.
  2. Top with whipped cream, if desired, and garnish with fresh strawberries.

3. Enjoy:

  1. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing strawberry milkshake!
