VANILLA MILKSHAKE

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Maraschino cherry (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Milkshake:

  1. In a blender, combine vanilla ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract.
  2. Blend on medium speed until smooth and creamy.
  3. If the milkshake is too thick, add a little more milk and blend again.

2. Serve the Milkshake:

  1. Pour the milkshake into a tall glass.
  2. Top with whipped cream, if desired, and garnish with a maraschino cherry.

3. Enjoy:

  1. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious vanilla milkshake!
