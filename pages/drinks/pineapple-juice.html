PINEAPPLE JUICE

Ingredients

  • 1 fresh pineapple
  • 1-2 tbsp sugar or honey (optional, depending on sweetness)
  • 2 cups cold water or coconut water
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Mint leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Pineapple:

  1. Peel and cut the fresh pineapple into chunks, removing the core.
  2. Place the pineapple chunks in a blender or juicer.

2. Blend the Pineapple Juice:

  1. Add the cold water or coconut water to the blender along with the pineapple.
  2. Blend until smooth. If the juice is too thick, add a little more water to adjust the consistency.
  3. Optional: Add sugar or honey for sweetness and blend again until combined.

3. Serve the Juice:

  1. Pour the fresh pineapple juice into glasses.
  2. Optional: Add ice cubes to chill the juice and garnish with mint leaves for a refreshing touch.

4. Enjoy:

  1. Serve immediately and enjoy your fresh homemade pineapple juice!
