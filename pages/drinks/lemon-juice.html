LEMON JUICE

Ingredients

  • 4-5 fresh lemons
  • 1/4 cup sugar (or to taste)
  • 3-4 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Mint leaves (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

1. Extract Lemon Juice:

  1. Cut the lemons in half and squeeze out the juice using a citrus juicer or your hands. Remove any seeds.

2. Prepare the Lemonade:

  1. In a pitcher, add the freshly squeezed lemon juice, sugar, and cold water.
  2. Stir well until the sugar is fully dissolved. You can adjust the sugar to your taste preference.
  3. If you prefer a chilled drink, add ice cubes or refrigerate the lemonade for 30 minutes.

3. Serve and Garnish:

  1. Pour the lemon juice into glasses.
  2. Optional: Garnish with fresh mint leaves for extra flavor and decoration.

4. Enjoy:

  1. Serve your refreshing homemade lemon juice and enjoy!
