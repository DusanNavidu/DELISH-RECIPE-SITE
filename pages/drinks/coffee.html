COFFEE

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1-2 tbsp ground coffee (depending on your strength preference)
  • Milk (optional, to taste)
  • Sugar (optional, to taste)

Instructions

1. Prepare the Coffee:

  1. Boil water in a kettle or saucepan.
  2. Place the ground coffee into your coffee filter, French press, or pour-over coffee maker.
  3. Once the water reaches boiling point, pour it over the coffee grounds slowly, ensuring an even extraction.
  4. If using a French press, let the coffee steep for 4-5 minutes before pressing down.

2. Customize Your Coffee:

  1. Add milk or cream to your coffee if desired, stirring to combine.
  2. Sweeten your coffee with sugar or another sweetener, if desired.

3. Serve and Enjoy:

  1. Pour the coffee into your favorite mug and enjoy!
